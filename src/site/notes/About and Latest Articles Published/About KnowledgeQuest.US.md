## What My Website Is About

This website features a selection of articles drawn from my **Obsidian Notes**—a personal collection of carefully researched writings on topics that capture my interest. It continues to grow as I explore new subjects and update older work. Through this site, I share parts of my ongoing research journey with readers who enjoy learning as much as I do.

**Research Tools**

- I use artificial intelligence to locate reliable information and refine my writing.
- Books and printed materials serve as trusted, in-depth sources.
- I verify facts across multiple references to ensure accuracy and balance.

**Writing Process**
